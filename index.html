About Me

I'm Nihar Bagkar, a business and technology enthusiast driven by entrepreneurship, innovation, and the evolving landscape of AI, sustainability, and fashion tech. Currently, I'm pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Business, Technology, and Entrepreneurship at NYU's Stern School of Business, where I explore the intersection of strategy, product development, and venture-building.

My experience spans leadership, finance, and product strategy. I've founded and led initiatives like Rapid Fire Esports, building a 1,000+ member gaming community, and FutureVibe Consulting, a college consulting service. At NYU, I co-lead the BTE Venture Studio, focusing on self-driven ventures and startup incubation. I've also contributed to the Product Management Club, analyzing business strategies for companies like Stripe. My background includes data analytics as well as intelligent waste management research.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm passionate about soccer, travel, and the evolving relationship between technology and creativity. Whether launching ventures, refining business strategies, or exploring AI's role in fashion and retail, I'm always seeking ways to drive meaningful innovation.
